About the job
We are looking for a detail-oriented and analytical Data Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting complex datasets to support data-driven decision-making across various business functions. This role requires a strong ability to translate raw data into meaningful insights that drive operational efficiency and business growth.



Responsibilities:

 • Gather, clean, and preprocess data from multiple sources to ensure accuracy and reliability.

 • Conduct exploratory data analysis and generate insights to support business strategy.

 • Develop and maintain dashboards, reports, and visualizations using Tableau, Power BI, Looker, or similar BI tools.

 • Write and optimize SQL queries to extract, transform, and analyze large datasets.

 • Utilize Python, R, or Excel for statistical analysis, forecasting, and automation.

 • Work closely with cross-functional teams, including engineering, marketing, finance, and product teams, to provide actionable insights.

 • Identify trends, anomalies, and key performance indicators (KPIs) to inform business decisions.

 • Support A/B testing and data-driven experiments to optimize processes and improve performance.

 • Maintain documentation for data analysis processes, metrics definitions, and reporting standards.

 • Ensure compliance with data privacy regulations and security best practices.



Requirements:

 • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Statistics, Data Science, Computer Science, Mathematics, Economics, or a related field.

 • Proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation.

 • Hands-on experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or Looker.

 • Strong analytical skills and the ability to interpret and present data-driven insights.

 • Experience with Python or R for data analysis, automation, and statistical modeling (preferred).

 • Understanding of data warehousing, ETL processes, and cloud-based data platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure is a plus).

 • Knowledge of A/B testing, predictive analytics, and machine learning is a bonus.

 • Strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and ability to work with large datasets.

 • Excellent communication skills to translate technical insights for non-technical stakeholders.



Preferred Qualifications:

 • Experience working with big data tools such as Spark, Hadoop, or Snowflake.

 • Familiarity with APIs and data automation processes.

 • Background in business intelligence, marketing analytics, financial analytics, or operations analytics.

 • Strong project management and stakeholder engagement experience.



Benefits:

 • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.

 • Flexible work environment (remote or hybrid options available).

 • Professional development and training opportunities.

 • Health benefits, retirement plans, and other perks based on company policies.
